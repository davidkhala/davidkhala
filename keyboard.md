## Input method
切换全角/半角：shift + space 

## Keychron k8 pro 
- Press [Fn]+[1]/[2]/[3] (for 4 seconds) and pair with device named "Keychron K8 Pro"
- 开关背光：[Fn]+[Tab]
- 切换灯光效果：[Fn]+[Q]  

### 游戏模式
以下指令不会被执行： 
- Windows键 
- [ALT] + [F4] 
- [ALT] + [TAB] 
- [CTRL] + [ESC] 
- [CTRL] + [ALT] + [DELETE] 

使用[Fn] + [F9]组合键 to switch mode
